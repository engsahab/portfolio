Forsah - Employment Platform

I worked on this project with my team as our graduation project. The goal was to create a recruitment platform that connects job seekers with employers in an easy and effective way. Our platform focuses on inclusion, making it simple for people with special needs to participate and find the right opportunities. My Summer Training Project. During my summer university training at King Fahd Medical City, my team and I designed and developed a flight ticket booking website.
